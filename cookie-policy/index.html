Cookie Policy

This website uses cookies solely to enable the hCaptcha service on our contact form. These cookies are necessary for distinguishing between human users and bots and for protecting our website from spam and abuse.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files that websites store on your device. They help websites remember user preferences and improve security and functionality.

What Cookies We Use

We use cookies provided by hCaptcha, which is a security service that helps us detect bots. These cookies:

  • Are set by the hCaptcha service when you use the contact form.
  • Do not collect personal information beyond what is necessary to verify your humanity.
  • May store anonymised data to improve hCaptcha's security functionality.

You can learn more about hCaptcha's data practices here:

https://www.hcaptcha.com/privacy

Managing Cookies

By continuing to use this site and submitting the contact form, you consent to the use of hCaptcha cookies. You can adjust your cookie preferences through your browser settings.
