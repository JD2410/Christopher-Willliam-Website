What I'm about

20 Years in the Trade. Built on Experience, Driven by Quality.

With over 20 years in the building and construction industry, I've had the privilege of working on a wide range of projects, from small home renovations to large-scale builds. My journey began on the tools, gaining hands-on experience in various trades before transitioning into project management. This background gives me a deep understanding of the construction process from start to finish, ensuring that every project runs smoothly, stays on budget, and meets the highest standards.

As a project manager, I bring together skilled trades, oversee timelines, and handle client expectations, ensuring that everything moves forward efficiently. I believe that clear communication and careful planning are the keys to a successful build. Whether it's coordinating contractors, troubleshooting unexpected challenges, or keeping clients informed, I'm committed to making the process as straightforward and stress-free as possible.

What sets me apart is my dedication to quality and my hands-on approach. I don't just manage from a distance—I stay involved, ensuring that every detail is up to standard. I take pride in delivering work that not only meets but exceeds expectations, whether it's a full home renovation, an extension, or a new build.

If you're looking for a project manager who understands construction inside and out, is committed to high-quality work, and believes in making the process as seamless as possible, I'd love to hear about your project.
