What I'm about

20 Years in the Trade. Built on Experience, Driven by Quality.

With over 25 years in the building and construction industry, I've had the privilege of working on a vast range of projects, from small home renovations to large scale complete builds. My journey began after university working on barn conversions in Devon where the passion for building was ignited. I gained hands-on experience in various trades before transitioning into complete project management. This background gives me an in-depth understanding of the building process from start to finish. This ensures that every project runs smoothly, stays on budget and meets the highest standards.

As a project manager I've gathered together like-minded tradesmen whose eye for details and quality meets the high standards we are known for. Clear communication and careful planning are the keys to a successful build. Whether it's coordinating contractors, troubleshooting unexpected challenges or keeping clients informed, we are committed to making the process as straightforward and stress free as possible.

What sets my team apart is their dedication to quality and our hands-on approach. I stay very much involved, picking up on details and I take great pride in delivering work that exceeds expectations, whether it's a full home renovation, an extension, kitchen and bathroom installation or a complete new build.
